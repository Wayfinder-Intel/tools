Overview

These tools are designed to run client-side in your browser. Two of them are local-only; the Map tool makes limited network requests for geocoding and map tiles.

Facebook Graph Builder (graph.html)

  • Data sent externally: None. All parsing and graph operations occur locally.
  • Local storage: Uses localStorage for saving “Sessions” and your graph state.
  • Exports: JSON/CSV/PNG exports are generated in-browser for download.
  • Third-party assets: D3 library (CDN). No tracking, no API calls.

Facebook Meta Mapper (map.html)

  • Data sent externally: Place-name text only is sent to OpenStreetMap’s Nominatim service to obtain coordinates (geocoding).
  • Map tiles: Loads OpenStreetMap tiles to display the map.
  • Local storage: Geocoding results are cached in localStorage to reduce repeat lookups.
  • Exports: JSON/CSV exports are generated locally based on your filtered/mapped view.
  • Third-party assets: Leaflet + MarkerCluster (CDN), OpenStreetMap tiles, OpenStreetMap Nominatim for geocoding.
  • Tip: Avoid including personal identifiers in the place text if you do not want them sent to OSM geocoding.

Email Guesser (email.html)

  • Data sent externally: None. Address generation and copy/export happen locally.
  • Third-party assets: None.

CDNs & Services Used

  • D3 (Graph Builder): JavaScript charting library served from a CDN.
  • Leaflet & MarkerCluster (Meta Mapper): Map libraries served from a CDN.
  • OpenStreetMap Tiles (Meta Mapper): Basemap imagery.
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim (Meta Mapper): Geocoding service for place-name queries.

No analytics beacons or tracking scripts are embedded in these pages.
